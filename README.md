# DT Business Analytics Assignment

## Overview

This assignment focuses on identifying and researching high-potential manufacturing and technology companies aligned with DeepThought’s “Federer company” framework.

## Included Files

* Final company research sheet
* Methodology document
* 1000 company scale-up strategy
* Hand-drawn research process sketch / mindmap

## Research Focus

The research mainly focuses on:

* Defence manufacturing
* Aerospace
* Industrial automation
* Robotics
* Precision engineering
* Specialty chemicals
* Biotechnology

## Research Process

Companies were shortlisted based on:

* Product and manufacturing focus
* Technical complexity
* Growth signals
* Industry potential
* Operational scale
* Founder-driven execution culture

## Tools Used

* Google Search
* LinkedIn
* Company websites
* AI tools for research assistance and organization
* Manual verification for important information
